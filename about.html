Hello, I'm Sridharan, a self-taught 3D artist from Namakkal, Tamil Nadu. With a year of industry experience and a degree in Multimedia Production Technology, I specialize in creating high-quality 3D models and assets using various software. My focus is on continuous learning and exploring new techniques to enhance my skills in 3D art.
My passion is deeply rooted in the dynamic and visually captivating fields of FX, Maya modeling, texturing, lookdev, lighting, rendering, and Houdini FX. These disciplines allow me to merge technical prowess with artistic flair, whether I'm crafting intricate simulations, infusing models with textures and lighting, or refining visual aesthetics for immersive scenes. Each project fuels my drive to innovate and push the boundaries of what's achievable in the dynamic world of 3D art.
